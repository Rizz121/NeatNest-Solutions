Our Upholstery Cleaning Process:

Assessment:

We begin by assessing the condition of your upholstered furniture to identify any stains, spots, or areas of concern. This allows us to determine the best approach and cleaning products to use.


Pre-Treatment:

Stubborn stains and spots are pre-treated with specialized cleaning solutions to effectively break down dirt, grime, and oils.


Deep Cleaning:

Using advanced upholstery cleaning techniques and equipment, we thoroughly clean your furniture upholstery. Our cleaning solutions penetrate deep into the fabric to lift dirt and stains without causing damage.


Extraction:

We use powerful extraction equipment to remove dirt, residue, and moisture from your upholstery, leaving it clean, fresh, and virtually dry to the touch.


Deodorization:

Upon request, we can apply deodorizing treatments to eliminate odors and leave your upholstery smelling fresh and clean.
